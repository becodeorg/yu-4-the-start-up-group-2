A Day At The Races

So we decided to go for an oldtimer to use for an advertisement for Mercedes-Benz. When we had the modeling and texture done I was looking for a story and wondered if there were any women driving those deadly machines back in the 1930's, and yes there were and more than one.

But one of the most famous was Helle Nice who became champion in the mixed formula 1 competition. Altough she drove for other brands we wanted to pay a small tribute to all those female drivers competing in a mixed competition. So we created a scene, like it would be at the time, when the race Paris-Madrid passes a small rural village.
